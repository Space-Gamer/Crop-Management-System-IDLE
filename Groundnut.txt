Crop:Groundnut
Required Temperature:26-34C
Rainfall requirement: 50-125 cm
Soil requirement: Peanuts prefer a well-drained soil with a sandy or sandy-clay subsoil. Crumbly soil is the key to a successful peanut crop
Soil pH level: 6.0-7.5
Yeild: 4.0-4.5 tonnes per hectare
Fertilizers required (per hectare): Manure (Framyard): 10 cartloads
                                    Calcium (CaO): 20 kg
Growth period: 120-150 days
Sowing time: 1st week of June

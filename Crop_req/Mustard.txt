Crop:Mustard
Required Temperature:10-25C
Rainfall requirement: 65-100 cm
Soil requirement: Light to heavy soil is good for mustard and rapeseed cultivation. Can be grown in all type of soil whereas loam to heavy soils are suitable.
Soil pH level: 6.0-7.5
Yeild: 1 tonne per hectare 
Fertilizers required (per hectare): Manure (Framyard): 7-12 tonnes
                                    Nitrogen:20 to 35 kg
                                    Phosphorous(P4O10): 30 to 35 kg
                                    Potash (K2O): 20-25 kg 

Growth period: 110-140 days
Sowing time: September-October

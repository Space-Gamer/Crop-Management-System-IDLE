Crop:Ragi
Required Temperature:22-34C
Rainfall requirement: 100 cm
Soil requirement: Ragi can be cultivated an a variety of soils such as loam to poor shallow upland soils with organic matter. However Black soils with good drainage , porous,light,sandy loam are considered to be ideal.
Soil pH level: 4.5-8.0
Yeild: 4.0-4.5 tonnes per hectare
Fertilizers required (per hectare): Manure (Framyard): 10-15 cartloads
                                    NPK: 50kg
Growth period: 90-120 days
Sowing time: May-June

Crop:Sugarcane
Required Temperature:18-42C
Rainfall requirement: 100-150 cm 
Soil requirement: Sugarcane can be grown in all types of soils ranging from sandy loam to clay loam soil.  It however thrives best on well-drained soils
Soil pH level: 3.5-4.5
Yeild: 60-70 tonnes per hectare
Fertilizers required (per hectare): Manure (Framyard): 10-15 cartloads
                                    Nitrogen:30-50 kg
Growth period: 90-180 days
Sowing time: Mid October (Annual crop)
